# Privacy Policy for DevStack

**Last Updated: May 27, 2026**

Your privacy is critically important to us. **DevStack** is built from the ground up as a privacy-first, offline-capable utility application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with your use of the application across mobile and desktop platforms.

---

## 1. Data Collection and Usage
**We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or user information.**

Because DevStack is a local developer utility tool, all operations, calculations, formatting, and transformations happen entirely on your local device.
* **No Server Uploads:** Any data you paste, enter, or generate inside the app (such as JSON payloads, JWT tokens, regular expressions, or cryptographic hashes) never leaves your device.
* **No Personal Information:** We do not ask for, require, or have access to your name, email address, IP address, device identifiers, or location data.

## 2. Device Permissions
Depending on the platform you are using (iOS, macOS, Android, or Windows), the app may request certain system permissions to function correctly. These permissions are used strictly for local operations:
* **Clipboard / Pasteboard Access:** Used purely to allow you to click "Paste" to input text or "Copy" to copy generated code snippets. Your clipboard data is never read silently or sent over the internet.
* **File Storage / Document Access:** Used only when you explicitly select a file to calculate its cryptographic hash (e.g., MD5/SHA256) or drop a file into the generator. Files are processed entirely in memory locally and are never stored or uploaded.

## 3. Third-Party Services and Analytics
* **No Analytics:** We do not use third-party analytics frameworks, tracking cookies, or user-behavior monitoring software (such as Google Analytics or Firebase Analytics).
* **No Advertisements:** DevStack contains no advertisements, meaning no ad networks are tracking your activity.
* **App Store Telemetry:** Standard, anonymized crash logs or installation statistics may be collected automatically by the platform providers (Apple App Store, Google Play Store, Microsoft Store) if you have opted-in to share them with developers via your operating system settings. We only view these as aggregated, non-identifiable statistics.

## 4. Children’s Privacy
Our application does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. DevStack is safe for all users and complies with global child privacy regulations, including COPPA and GDPR.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect structural changes in the application. However, our core philosophy will never change: **we will never collect your data.** Any updates will be posted directly on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
